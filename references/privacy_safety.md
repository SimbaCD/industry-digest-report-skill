# Privacy and Safety

This public skill package must not contain private project material.

## Do Not Include

- personal names, phone numbers, email addresses, or office addresses;
- client names or matter names;
- private public-account lists;
- WeRSS database files;
- article exports from private projects;
- cookies, tokens, headers, QR screenshots, or login material;
- local absolute paths from a specific user's machine;
- historical outputs or final reports from an internal project.

## Browser and Web Access

Use browser/Web Access only when the user explicitly asks or when operating a user-controlled local service such as WeRSS UI.

Always pause for:

- login;
- QR authorization;
- captcha;
- account confirmation;
- payment or subscription gates.

Never:

- extract or print cookies/tokens;
- automate captcha solving;
- bypass anti-crawl controls;
- scrape WeChat pages silently as the default collection method.

## Publication Safety

Before publishing, remove:

- generation timestamps;
- local script traces;
- debug labels;
- internal notes;
- source paths;
- draft-only reviewer comments.
